Inverter Pump Label

Technical specification translation, for import/export business.

    This project involved translating technical specifications of a pump label from Dutch-English for an import/export business. Accuracy is critical because the labels are be scrutinised by customs officials when the product is sent over the border. We were able to complete this job on time, meeting the client's required technical standard.

  • Category: Technical Translation

Ehre Sanitär Website

Website translation from Dutch to English.

Translation of a wash basin wholesaler website from Dutch to English, in which it was imperative to capture the client's specific message towards prospective customers. In order to capture description of design nuances and technical designations, industry research was done to ensure accuracy.

  • Client: Ehre Sanitär
  • Category: Website Translation

Filmster Gezocht

Transcription and Translation of a Dutch TV show.

The client requested a full transcription and translation of a 40 minute Dutch television programme, with specific instructions to capture the gist of what was going on, rather than maintain a purely literal translation. Because this text would be further translated into Chinese, it was imperative that subsequent translators were unburdened by highly specific cultural references, but were still able to capture the tone and humor of the show. Successful delivery of a 5000 word transcription, plus >5000 words of translated text were delivered and approved within 24 hours.

  • Category: Media Translation and Transcription
